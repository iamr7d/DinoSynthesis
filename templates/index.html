About this model — β-VAE v3 (Balanced)

A β-VAE (β = 2.0) trained on 1,278 balanced tensors — 426 per anchor group — across 11 phylogenetic classes (Tinamou × 2, Cassowary, Emu, Kiwi, Ostrich, Rhea, Crocodylia, Elephantidae, Phocoidea, Whippomorpha). A strategic undersampling protocol eliminated the 24:1 Bird-to-Reptile bias, improving validation reconstruction loss from 0.0714 → 0.065539 (+8.2%). Trained for 95 epochs (early-stopped) with KL warm-up (40 epochs), free-bits floor 0.5 nats, and a group-weighted sampler ensuring each batch is exactly 1/3 Bird · 1/3 Reptile · 1/3 Mass. The bird–crocodile midpoint in latent space approximates the acoustic 'DNA' of a stem archosaur (†non-avian dinosaur) before body-mass scaling.
